## 🏁 How to Use the Dashboard

1. **Read a chapter** — It will be automatically marked as "read"
2. **Mark chapters complete** — Check the box at the bottom of each chapter page
3. **Take practice tests** — Mark them as completed on the dashboard
4. **Track progress** — The sidebar progress bar follows you everywhere

Your data is saved in your browser's local storage. It will persist across sessions but clearing your browser data will reset it.
